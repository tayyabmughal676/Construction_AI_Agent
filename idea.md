# Multi-Domain AI Agent 

## Departments
1. Construction
2. Manufacturing
3. HR


# Future Features
Construction
• Predictive Safety: Analyze site data (like images or sensor readings) to predict and flag potential safety hazards before they cause accidents.
• Automated Progress Monitoring: Use drone footage or 360-degree photos to automatically track construction progress against the project schedule and create reports.
• Smart Resource Allocation: Optimize the allocation of equipment and personnel across different tasks and project sites based on real-time needs and availability.
• AI-Powered Cost Estimation: Improve bidding accuracy by using historical project data to generate more precise cost and timeline estimates.
Manufacturing
• Predictive Maintenance: Monitor machinery health through sensor data to predict failures and schedule maintenance proactively, minimizing downtime.
• Supply Chain Optimization: Forecast demand for raw materials, predict potential supply chain disruptions, and suggest alternative suppliers to ensure production continuity.
• Quality Control Automation: Use computer vision to inspect products on the assembly line in real-time, identifying defects that might be missed by human inspectors.
HR
• Intelligent Talent Acquisition: Automate the screening of resumes to identify the best candidates and use chatbots to handle initial interview scheduling.
• Personalized Employee Onboarding: Create a customized onboarding experience for new hires, guiding them through necessary training and paperwork.
• Workforce Analytics: Analyze employee data to identify trends in turnover, forecast future hiring needs, and get insights into overall team productivity and morale.
